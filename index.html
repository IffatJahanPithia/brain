Why Us

We believe that questioning our own brain is key to establishing the efficient use of it. We introduce the tools and techniques to ignite the power of your brain through interactive brain engaging practical sessions. The brain engaging technique is applied to learning skills that are essential in the workplace. We invite you to join the journey to engage your own brain to learn the life skills you need to succeed in life.

OUR Lead TRAINER

Educated in the UK, Prof Islam has over 40 years of teaching experience. To enhance his teaching skills through the use of technology he did a Master’s in Instructional Design Technology (MIDT) from Open University Malaysia (OUM) where he also became an international facilitator for the subject of Human Performance Technology (HPT) and recognized by his students as the best online teacher. He has designed a teaching and workshop methodology called Neuron Engaging Teaching Techniques (NETT). The methodology draws on Verses from the Al-Qur’an that deal with how the brain works and over 30 years of action research on how best to implement brain-based learning

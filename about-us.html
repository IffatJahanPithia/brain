Who we are

It is no longer an opinion, but a fact that most employers are facing a huge skills gap- they do not find the employees they look for. They look for employees who can think independently in the labor market - employees who can think independently. The problem lies in the fact that soft skills are a fundamental prerequisite to any sort of learning. We have built a solution designed to fill the gap left by our traditional education system through various brain engaging techniques.

What is our purpose

Our purpose is to mitigate the gap between employees and companies. To make sure participants learn during the training sessions, retaining the lesson has to be established in a meaningful way. Support them to become lifelong learners. We aspire to create lifelong learners to support them to solve life problems that a company requires.

Our Philosophy

Thinking and questioning are the differentiators between man and other species. This is the key to everything. Link Le Brain employs a unique approach that engages each participant to elevate their level of confidence to “self-train” and “self-teach” with light coaching from the moderator while they go through a series of classes. With our practices, we aim to challenge old assumptions about modes of instruction and open up exciting new possibilities for rethinking-centered learning.

 

Our Vision

To create awareness among the learners to believe in themselves and improve their self-esteem and capacity to do anything. .

Our Goals

  • Mitigate the gap between the company requirements and employee
  • Developing lifelong skills
  • Apply meaning to everything
  • Promoting thinking and questioning
  • Provide the necessary skills to face the challenges of 21st Century
  • Utilize facilities beyond the school/work to be used differently to enhance learning
